To a New Year belong wishes
of health and happiness,
especially to those people
who help me piece by piece.
A New Year includes wishes
and sometimes even a fine feast,
which is why so many people
toast with a glass of wine.
To a New Year belong wishes
preferably full of color and also full of light,
which I bring you best
with a happy and joyful face.
To a New Year belong wishes
very personal and special,
for you (you) all the best!
for me you(your) remain: ideal!!!
